# PRODIGY_DS_01
This project explores Spotify user data to reveal insights into demographics, listening habits, and preferences. Findings offer trends by age and gender, popular genres, and peak listening times, and help identify user behavior.
Project Summary: Spotify User Survey Analysis
Details:
This project analyzes survey data on Spotify users' demographics, listening habits, preferences, and attitudes toward various platform aspects. The dataset includes users' age, gender, subscription plans, preferred listening devices, favorite genres, podcast preferences, and listening behaviors.

Purpose:
The goal is to gain insights into Spotify users' behavior patterns, identify key trends, and explore relationships between variables (such as age and preferred music genre, or gender and subscription plan). These insights can help Spotify or other streaming services better understand their audience, improve user experience, and tailor content or marketing strategies to specific user segments.

Scope:
This analysis covers:

Demographics: Age and gender distribution of Spotify users.
Usage Patterns: Frequency of music and podcast listening, and preferred times for listening.
Content Preferences: Favorite genres, content types (music vs. podcasts), and preferred podcast duration.
Subscription Behavior: Popularity of different subscription plans and users' willingness to continue with premium.
Discovery and Influence: How users discover new music and what moods or situations influence their listening choices.
Using data cleaning, visualization, and exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques, we aim to uncover meaningful insights from the dataset.

Conclusion:
The analysis will reveal key trends, such as:

Popular genres and listening devices across demographics.
Differences in premium subscription willingness by age or gender.
Preferred content and usage times, can guide Spotify’s content and marketing decisions.
In summary, this task provides a comprehensive overview of Spotify user preferences, enabling data-driven decisions to enhance user engagement and satisfaction on the platform.
